How to Cook Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sliced onions
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Arrange the pepperoni slices, mushrooms, bell peppers, and onions on top of the cheese.
  7. Sprinkle dried oregano and basil over the toppings.
  8. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  9. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.
  10. Serve and enjoy your homemade pizza!
